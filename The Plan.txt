Game Idea: Bubble Tea Maker

Mechanics: Control a hand and to make a cup of bubble tea. Pick up ingredients and place them inside a cup in a specific order to maximum score. Ability to pick up the cup and shake to mix the bubble tea.

Ingredient List:
- Tapioca
- Tea (Black or Green)
- Sugar
- Milk
- Jelly
- Ice

Flavours
- Mango
- Strawberry
- Taro
- HoneyDew
- Lychee
- Peach
